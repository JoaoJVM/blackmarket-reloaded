Blackmarket-Reloaded is called an incremental-game. This game is totally text-based and doesn't have any graphics.

Please note that the game is currently made to be run on small screens (like tablets) and medium/big screens (like computers) but not on your phone!

Actions :
  • Level-up your actions to earn more dollars.
  • Unlock new actions, they will earn a massive amount of dollars!
  • Earn reputation points and level-up!
  • Upgrade your actions via the "Research center" tab to earn more and more dollars! (accessible on the menu).

Prestige :
  • With the prestige system, you restart everything from zero, but you earn experience.
  • Your experience determine your rank, which will determine your rank multiplier.
  • Rank multiplier acts as a global income multiplier, it multiplies every income you can have in the game!

Achievements :
  • Earn achievements, they give you awesome boosts!
  • The game is finished when you reach all the achievements.
