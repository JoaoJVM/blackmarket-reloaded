Need help?

Please note that the game is made to be run on small screens (like tablets) and medium/big screens (like computers) but not on your phone!

Actions :
  • Level-up your actions to earn more dollars.
  • Unlock new actions, they will earn a massive amount of dollars!
  • Earn reputation points and level-up!
  • Upgrade your actions via the "Research center" tab to earn more and more dollars! (accessible on the menu).

Prestige :
  • With the prestige system, you restart everything from zero, but you earn experience.
    Please note : this is called a soft-reset.

Collections :
  • Collections are items you keep even when you prestige (soft-reset).
  • There are a lot of different collections, each one give you random bonus on a specific thing.
  • Each collections is different, they all give different bonus type.
  • You can find items on collection by earning achievements and buying upgrades at the research-center.

Achievements :
  • Earn achievements, they give you awesome boosts!
  • Try to find items for your collections.
  • The game is finished when you earn all the achievements.
