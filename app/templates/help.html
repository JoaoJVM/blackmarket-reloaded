Blackmarket-Reloaded is called an incremental-game, it's a game with text without graphics. For some people, it's hard to call this a game.

Actions :
  • Level-up your actions to earn more dollars.
  • Unlock new actions, they will earn a massive amount of dollars.
  • Upgrade your actions via the "Upgrades" tab (accessible on the menu).

Production :
  • First, to use the production system you need to invest on it.
  • Sell drugs : weed, meth, cocaine.
  • Buy builds to produce a certain amount of the drug wanted and hire dealers to sell it.
  • Upgrade your drug price/g on the "Upgrades" tab.

Prestige :
  • With the prestige system, you restart everything from zero, but you earn experience.
  • Your experience determine your rank, which will determine your rank multiplier
  • Rank multiplier acts as a global income multiplier, it multiplies every income you can have in the game.
